Vishwa Ayurveda

According to Ayurveda, every individual is born with a unique combination of doshas termed as PRAKRUTI (body’s physical constitution) that is determined at the moment of conception and influenced by seasonal variations and several other internal and external factors.
We are all different, everyone inherent strength and weakness. If you know yours and know how your constitution causes them, you can adjust for them and make your life happier and healthier.
